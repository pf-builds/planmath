Why this exists

Most health benefits decisions get made with incomplete numbers, a premium here, a deductible there, no clear total. These tools do the actual math in one place, using the same logic that decides real claims. Nothing here is tax, legal, or insurance advice, and each calculator says exactly what it doesn't account for. Confirm specifics with your plan administrator before making a final decision.
